Resilience as a Business Imperative

Continuity planning is no longer about compliance—it's about safeguarding reputation, operations, and stakeholder trust in the face of disruption. Ignasia Consulting delivers end-to-end Business Continuity Management (BCM) and Crisis Response solutions for regulated industries, high-growth companies, and organizations looking to future-proof their operations.

Key Service Components

  • BCP/DRP Strategy & Roadmaps: Design and implement policies, frameworks, and roles aligned with ISO 22301, RBI/SEBI guidelines, and best practice.
  • Business Impact Analysis (BIA): Detailed analysis to identify critical processes, dependencies, RTO/RPO, and financial/operational impacts.
  • Scenario-based Risk Assessment: Analysis for cyber-attacks, natural disasters, pandemic, supply chain, infrastructure, and personnel threats.
  • Continuity & Recovery Playbooks: Step-by-step plans for crisis identification, containment, recovery, and communication—test-ready and executable.
  • Crisis Simulation & Tabletop Exercises: Customized drills and simulations to validate plans, train teams, and highlight real-world improvement areas.
  • Vendor & Supply Chain Continuity: Third-party risk mapping, contracts review, and failover procedures for mission-critical partners.
  • Awareness, Training, and Review: Targeted training for executives, operational leaders, and staff, plus annual review cycles for continuous improvement.

Methodology Overview

  1. Discovery & BIA: Workshops and data gathering to identify business/disaster scenarios, critical assets, dependencies, and maximum tolerable downtime.
  2. Risk Assessment: Evaluation of single-point-of-failure, vulnerabilities, and the likelihood/impact of diverse disruptions.
  3. Planning & Solution Design: Draft bespoke plans—role assignments, escalation paths, IT systems, process alternatives, and recovery priorities.
  4. Testing & Simulation: Live tabletop exercises, incident walk-throughs, and real-time evaluation of team readiness.
  5. Implementation & Training: Guidance as new plans are adopted, training at the executive and staff levels, and simulation debriefs.
  6. Review & Optimization: Regular reviews after real or simulated incidents and whenever there are organizational/IT changes or regulations.

Expected Outcomes

  • Up-to-date, practical BCP/DRP documentation (test-ready)
  • Clear roles and escalation procedures for any crisis
  • Annual simulation/test program with executive dashboard reporting
  • Reduced recovery times, minimized loss, and safeguarded customer trust
  • Full compliance with RBI, SEBI, ISO 22301, and sectoral standards
  • Resilient supply chain and third-party controls
  • Continuous readiness—responsive to business and threat landscape changes

Industry Application

  • BFSI, NBFCs, insurance, and fintech
  • Healthcare/hospitals and pharma supply chain
  • IT, SaaS, cloud-native operations, and manufacturing
  • Critical infrastructure and government services
