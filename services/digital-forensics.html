Act Fast. Investigate Deep. Recover Strong.

Ignasia Consulting’s Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR) service helps organizations respond to cyber incidents, breaches, and regulatory inquiries with speed, accuracy, and legal assurance. We combine robust investigative methods, world-class tools, and experience across financial, health, and technology sectors to provide clarity and support when you need it most.

Core Service Components

  • Incident Response Planning: DFIR playbook preparation, tabletop exercises, and role-based scenario simulations.
  • Evidence Collection & Preservation: Forensic imaging, chain-of-custody controls, and best-practice documentation for courts and regulators.
  • Attack Investigation: Endpoint and network forensics, threat hunting, root cause analysis, and variant mapping for malware and ransomware cases.
  • Regulatory Support: Regulator-ready breach reports (DPDP, GDPR, RBI), notification assistance, eDiscovery, and liaison support.
  • Remediation & Recovery: Containment, eradication, system rebuilding, user notification, and lessons-learned workshops.

Methodology

  1. Preparation: Develop incident response plans and crisis communication flows; ensure forensic readiness.
  2. Detection & Analysis: Use advanced monitoring and detection for rapid event identification; triage event severity and systems affected.
  3. Evidence Acquisition: Secure volatile and non-volatile data (RAM, disk, logs, network captures) using industry-standard forensic tools.
  4. Investigation: Deep dive into artifacts, timelines, and anomaly detection. Reconstruct attacker actions, persistence, and exfiltration.
  5. Containment, Eradication & Recovery: Isolate impacted assets, block attacker channels, guide clean-up and secure restore steps.
  6. Reporting: Deliver actionable, regulator-ready reports including root-cause, timelines, executive summaries, and technical annexes.
  7. Post-Incident: Facilitate lessons-learned, update playbooks, run retesting and awareness programs.

Expected Outcomes

  • Minimized breach impact and swift return to business-as-usual
  • Clear root-cause understanding for leadership and regulators
  • Evidence trails and documentation suitable for legal proceedings
  • Uplifted preparedness and team confidence for future events

  • Healthcare, pharma, health-tech
  • SaaS, digital businesses
  • Manufacturing & supply chain
