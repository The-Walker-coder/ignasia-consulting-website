A Strategic Approach to GRC

At Ignasia Consulting, we believe Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) must be business enablers—not obstacles. Our GRC advisory service integrates risk policy, compliance frameworks, and assurance methodologies, delivering practical solutions that align with strategy and culture while meeting the highest regulatory standards.

Key Service Offerings

  • GRC Framework Development: Establish holistic governance and risk operating models using ISO 31000, COSO, and NIST guidance.
  • Policy and Procedure Design: Tailored policies and SOPs for IT, cyber, operational, and financial risk domains; mapping regulations (RBI/SEBI/IRDAI/DPDP).
  • Risk Assessment and Risk Registers: Quantitative/qualitative techniques, control mapping, and dashboards for real-time visibility.
  • Compliance Automation: Automated control monitoring, compliance evidence tools, and audit workflow digitalization.
  • Internal Audit & Assurance: Full lifecycle IT/cyber/operational audits, control self-assessments, and continuous assurance reporting.
  • Regulatory Readiness: Regulatory horizon scanning, impact analysis across key Indian and global laws, and board reporting frameworks.

Sample Methodology

  1. Current State Review: Evaluate existing governance, risk, and compliance structures; maturity assessment vs. best-practices.
  2. Stakeholder Risk Workshop: Facilitate leadership and risk owner workshops to define appetite, tolerance, and culture.
  3. Framework & Policy Development: Draft and operationalize integrated GRC frameworks; align policies with regulations and business realities.
  4. Risk Register & Controls Mapping: Develop enterprise risk registers, map to business processes, assign ownership, and establish KRI/KCI dashboards.
  5. Implementation & Uplift: Execute remediation actions, deliver awareness training, and implement compliance automation tools.
  6. Continuous Improvement: Set up regular review cycles, audit schedules, and establish feedback loops bridging leadership and operational teams.

Expected Outcomes

  • Strengthened and clearly documented governance structure
  • Consolidated risk and compliance dashboards for board/c-suite
  • Regulatory alignment with fewer audit findings and less remediation
  • Empowered staff and leadership with clear policies and digital tools
  • Agile, repeatable risk and compliance workflows that scale as your organization grows

Industry Application

  • Financial services (BFSI, NBFCs)
  • Insurance and healthcare
  • Fintech, SaaS, technology
  • Manufacturing and global supply chain
