Turn Uncertainty into Operational Confidence

Business disruptions are inevitable—be it cyberattacks, natural disasters, pandemics, or operational failures. Ignasia Consulting’s business continuity and crisis management services help you navigate uncertainty, minimize downtime, and recover effectively—no matter what comes your way.

Comprehensive Service Offerings

  • Business Impact Analysis (BIA): Mapping critical functions, interdependencies, and tolerance thresholds.
  • Continuity Strategy & BCP Development: Creation and implementation of practical, customized continuity plans.
  • Disaster Recovery (DR) Planning: Technology, data, and infrastructure recovery strategies for IT and operations.
  • Crisis Management & Communication: Playbooks, team formation, scenario messaging, and reputation management.
  • Training & Tabletop Exercises: Real-world simulations and drills for staff, crisis teams, and leadership.
  • Regulatory Alignment: Compliance with RBI, SEBI, IRDAI, ISO 22301, and other standards as required.
  • Continuous Maturity Uplift: Ongoing gap analysis, lessons-learned review, and BCMS process improvement.

Methodology Snapshot

  1. Discovery & Stakeholder Engagement: Identify business priorities, key operations, and recovery time objectives (RTO/RPO).
  2. Impact & Vulnerability Assessment: Analyze risks, single points of failure, and potential loss scenarios.
  3. Document Response Plans: Develop BCP/DRP for critical processes, suppliers, technology, and vital staff roles.
  4. Plan Testing & Simulation: Facilitate walkthroughs, simulations, and crisis communication drills with all impacted teams.
  5. Program Deployment: Train personnel, assign plan owners, and establish regular review/maintenance schedules.
  6. Continuous Improvement: Post-incident review, update documentation, and strengthen organizational resilience.

Expected Client Outcomes

  • Rapid, coordinated recovery from incidents—minimizing financial and reputational loss
  • Clear, actionable plans for crisis, disaster, and operational disruption
  • Improved compliance posture (ISO 22301/industry, regulator)
  • Embedded continuity culture from front-line to C-suite
  • Increased customer, partner, and board confidence
  • Measurable improvement in resilience maturity year-on-year

Sector Experience

  • Health, Pharma, and Life Sciences
  • Manufacturing/Supply Chain
  • Technology, SaaS, Cloud, and Digital-first organizations
