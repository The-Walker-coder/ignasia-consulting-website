Behavioral Change. Lasting Resilience. Measurable Outcomes.

Ignasia Consulting delivers tailored training and capacity building programs, equipping teams to understand, anticipate, and defend against cyber and operational risks. Our curriculum balances real-world scenarios, regulatory requirements, and human psychology for maximum impact—whether for C-suite, developers, or frontline staff.

Training & Enablement Programs

  • Information Security Awareness: Staff and executive seminars, Phishing and social engineering simulation, and “security-first” culture development.
  • GRC & Compliance Training: Risk management, regulatory compliance (RBI/SEBI/IRDAI/DPDP), and privacy workshops.
  • Incident Response & Tabletop Drills: Live-play and virtual crisis exercises tested on your environment, roles, and tools.
  • Cybersecurity for Developers: Secure coding techniques, secure SDLC, API and DevSecOps best-practices.
  • Data Protection & Privacy by Design: Sector-specific privacy education and DSR process role-play.
  • Custom Role-Specific Programs: Third-party/vendor, finance, HR, operations and board briefings.

How We Deliver

  1. Needs Assessment: Identify audience, risk posture, industry, and regulatory drivers.
  2. Content Tailoring: Align modules and scenarios to everyday risks and strategic objectives.
  3. Interactive Learning: Blended live/virtual, simulation software, hands-on “red vs blue” exercises.
  4. Assessment & Certification: Pre- and post-training measurement, engagement analytics, attendance tracking, and certificates.
  5. Retention & Reporting: Longitudinal knowledge checks, awareness campaigns, and ROI reporting for management and regulators.

What You Achieve

  • Significant reduction in social engineering and compliance incidents
  • Demonstrable compliance with ISO 27001, DPDP, and RBI/IRDAI/SEBI mandates
  • Security-minded culture and self-reporting behaviors throughout the org
  • Board and audit-ready evidence of ongoing human risk mitigation
