From Readiness to Certification—and Beyond

ISO 27001 is the global gold standard for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS). Ignasia Consulting guides clients from first assessment to certification, and onwards into culture change and continual improvement. We combine technical depth, business focus, and proven methods—delivering not just compliance, but confidence for your board, regulators, and customers.

Comprehensive ISO 27001 Services

  • Readiness Assessment: Gap analysis against ISO 27001:2022, focusing on assets, controls, culture, and business priorities.
  • Scoping & Context: Defining ISMS boundaries, assets, internal/external issues, and risk environment.
  • Risk Assessment & Treatment: Asset-based risk methodologies, risk registers, custom scoring models, and treatment plans.
  • Policy & Procedure Development: All mandatory ISO policies (from Information Security Policy to supplier security, BYOD, incident response, and more).
  • Controls Implementation: Full coverage for Annex A controls—technical/organizational/physical. Automation and documentation support especially for SMEs and digital-era teams.
  • Awareness & Training: Board to staff security education, social engineering resilience, and role-based content.
  • Internal Audit & Management Review: Audit checklist, findings management, practice for certification interviews and evidence requirements.
  • Certification Assistance & Ongoing Support: Pre-certification readiness, auditor selection, closeout of non-conformities, and long-term continuous improvement.

Step-by-Step Methodology

  1. Initial Assessment: Stakeholder interviews, documentation review, risk context analysis.
  2. ISMS Design: Build documentation suite, nominate ISMS/owners, define scope.
  3. Implementation Workshops: Controls mapping, evidence gathering, and process walkthroughs (technical and non-technical).
  4. Training & Evidence: Deliver targeted awareness, simulate incidents, and collect evidence of control operation.
  5. Internal Audit: Conduct audit as per ISO 27001:2022 requirements, document findings and corrective actions.
  6. Management Review & Certification Prep: Review meeting, update statement of applicability, and close gaps.
  7. External Certification: Liaise with certifying body, assist with the audit, address final questions, and support in closing non-conformities.
  8. Continual Improvement: Metrics dashboards, recurring risk review, and annual audit planning.

Typical Outcomes

  • First-time ISO 27001 certification—on time, on budget
  • Reduced security incidents and improved risk posture
  • Stronger data governance, vendor oversight, and regulatory confidence
  • Ongoing ISMS maturity: keeping pace with threats and business change
  • Measurable ROI: improved win rates, audit readiness, market entry, and customer trust
