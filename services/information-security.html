Building Trust, One Secure Step at a Time

Ignasia Consulting delivers end-to-end information security strategies, frameworks, and implementation services tailored to your organization’s size, sector, and risk profile. We help you build resilience against threats, meet regulatory demands, and drive a culture of proactive security.

Key Service Areas

  • Security Risk Assessments & Gap Analysis: Threat modeling, vulnerability analysis, cloud/OT/IOT reviews, third-party risk.
  • ISMS & ISO 27001 Implementation: Scoping, risk context, asset registers, policy drafting, controls, awareness.
  • Framework Adoption: NIST CSF, CIS Controls, RBI/IRDAI/SEBI/ISO regulations for India and global markets.
  • Policy Formulation & Training: Employee/information use policy, social engineering resilience, secure SDLC.
  • DevSecOps & Secure Cloud: Secure architecture, cloud reviews, DevSecOps integration for modern teams.
  • Security Audits & Incident Response: Hands-on audits, incident response planning, forensic readiness.

Sample Methodology

  1. Discovery & Scoping: Engage with client leadership; understand business processes, risk appetite, compliance needs.
  2. Threat/Risk Assessment: Conduct asset inventories, threat modeling (physical, digital, procedural), and vulnerability scans.
  3. Gap Analysis: Map against ISO 27001, NIST, and regulatory frameworks; identify critical control weaknesses.
  4. Roadmap Development: Prioritize remediation, design custom control sets, and deliver tailored implementation schedule.
  5. Execution & Enablement: Deploy controls, conduct training, shape policies; support incident readiness and testing.
  6. Continuous Monitoring & Review: Define ongoing metrics, dashboards, and regular audit check-ins for sustained maturity.

Expected Outcomes

  • ISO 27001/ISMS or NIST readiness, certification support
  • Board-level risk visibility and clear reporting for stakeholders
  • Measurably reduced vulnerabilities and audit findings
  • Enhanced security culture—from the front-line to leadership
  • Regulatory peace of mind (RBI/IRDAI/SEBI/privacy regimes)
  • Process and technology improvements that scale as you grow

Industry Focus

  • Banking and financial services (BFSI)
  • Insurance, fintech, and NBFCs
  • Healthcare, pharma, and digital health
  • Manufacturing, logistics, auto/industrial
  • SaaS, technology, and cloud-centric organizations
