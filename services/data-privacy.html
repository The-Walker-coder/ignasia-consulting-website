Turning Compliance into Competitive Advantage

Privacy and data protection are no longer just legal requirements. They’re brand differentiators and core elements of customer loyalty. Ignasia Consulting helps organizations achieve compliance (GDPR, DPDP Act), build “privacy by design,” and prepare for regulatory scrutiny and consumer trust demands.

Key Service Offerings

  • Privacy Gap Assessments: Maturity assessment against GDPR, India DPDP, and sectoral privacy regulations.
  • Data Mapping & Flow Diagrams: End-to-end mapping of personal and sensitive data, processors, and cross-border flows.
  • Privacy by Design: Embedding privacy controls into application and business process life cycles.
  • Policy & Consent Management: User permissions, disclosures, and consent handling for customers and staff.
  • Data Subject Rights Management: Enabling right to access, correction, deletion, data portability, and response workflows.
  • Training & Awareness: Role-based programs for frontline staff, developers, and DPOs.
  • Incident Readiness: Data breach response, notification planning, regulatory handling, and DPIA/PIA facilitation.

Sample Methodology

  1. Discovery & Scoping: Clarify regulatory context, business structure, and sensitive data types (PII, SPI, health, financial).
  2. Data Lifecycle Audit: Assess collection, storage, processing, access, sharing, and disposal for regulatory gaps.
  3. Risk Assessment & DPIA: Conduct Data Protection and Privacy Impact Assessments to quantify and mitigate risk exposure.
  4. Policy Development: Draft privacy policy, internal guidelines, processor agreements, breach communications, and consent language.
  5. Control Implementation: Deploy technical and organizational controls: encryption, minimization, logging, approvals, and monitoring.
  6. Training & Ongoing Review: Deliver awareness, table-top breach tests, and regular privacy audits.

Expected Outcomes

  • GDPR and DPDP compliance (with documented evidence and board packs)
  • Reduction in privacy incidents and regulatory findings
  • Improved consumer and B2B trust, driving business growth
  • Ensured cross-border data transfer legality and readiness for audits
  • Staff and vendor awareness uplift for privacy obligations
  • Sustainable, operationalized privacy risk management

Industries & Application

  • Banking, insurance, fintech, and regulated BFSI
  • Healthcare, pharma, clinical research, and MedTech
  • eCommerce, SaaS, and digital marketplace operators
  • Employers handling large-scale employee or client personal data
