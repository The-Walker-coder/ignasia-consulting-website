Proactive, Measurable Enterprise Risk Management

Risk is not just about threats—it’s about opportunity and resilience. Ignasia Consulting equips organizations with advanced enterprise risk management (ERM) practices: from robust frameworks and quantification to hands-on mitigation plans, aligned to regulatory reality and board needs.

Key Service Offerings

  • ERM Program Development: Framework (ISO 31000/COSO), policies, risk taxonomy, and processes tailored to your sector.
  • Risk Register & Assessment: Comprehensive qualitative and quantitative risk identification, scoring, and documentation, with clear risk owners and KRI/KCI tracking.
  • Risk Quantification: Business impact analysis using scenario modeling, Monte Carlo simulation, and loss forecasts where appropriate.
  • Regulatory Risk Programs: RBI/SEBI/IRDAI, DPDP Act, privacy, and sectoral compliance risk assessments; gap mapping and reporting.
  • Operational & IT Risk: End-to-end assessment, controls mapping, incident and loss event cataloging, and supply chain risk analysis.
  • Incident, Crisis & BCP Integration: Risk-driven scenario testing, tabletop exercises, and live simulations (see also BCP services).

Sample Methodology

  1. Kickoff & Stakeholder Engagement: Work with leadership to align risk appetite, set objectives, and clarify critical assets.
  2. Risk Identification & Analysis: Map business processes, gather data, conduct workshops, and use tools to identify risks and root causes.
  3. Assessment & Quantification: Apply risk matrices and simulation software for impact and likelihood ratings, integrating controls analysis.
  4. Register & Remediation: Build an actionable, living risk register; assign mitigation actions and deadlines.
  5. Reporting & Oversight: Deliver board-ready dashboards, executive summaries, and regulatory submissions.
  6. Continuous Monitoring: Periodic review cycles, update KRIs, and align with your organization’s risk culture and business changes.

Expected Outcomes

  • Board-level visibility into key risks and mitigation status
  • Risk-informed business planning and investment decisions
  • Regulatory compliance confidence—fewer violations and audit gaps
  • Mature, automated risk processes—repeatable and scalable
  • Embedded risk-aware culture throughout teams
  • Preparedness for operational disruptions, cyber events, or compliance crises

Industry Expertise

  • Banking/Finance, NBFCs, and Insurance
  • Healthcare & Pharma
  • Manufacturing, Logistics, and Technology
  • Fintech, e-commerce, SaaS, and digital-first businesses
